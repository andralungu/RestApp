Our Story

Our team is composed of three young enthusiasts with different social, economical, cultural and educational backgrounds, but with a common target: to decrease the level of poverty while raising social awareness among the users of our website.

Oftenly, we hear ourselves imply that, even though there are millions of disabled people in our country, there is little or no support provided for them either by the Government or by any other non-profit Institution.

One example would be the infinite queues that usually form in front of the special door of the T.G.V. that goes to Paris simply because the SNCF provides only one assistant to help the clients with mobility issues mount the stairs. If a student(or any other individual disposing of several minutes of spare time) was aware of this situation, he/she would surely offer his/her services to provide aid for these citizens in exchange for a small fee or even free of charge.

This is what GetMoving! is basically about! We struggle to build pathways towards a healthier, friendlier social environment by providing an online platform where people can seek assistance among other reliable members of the community.
